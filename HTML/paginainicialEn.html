FLORIDAAP EXPLICATION

Welcome to FloridApp, a page where you can easily manage processes that we can find on a regular basis as a student/teacher/administrator at Florida University, we go with the explanation of the different functions of the menu that we can find on the left:





Meeting:

In the Meeting section we can find, first, a selection of all the modules available in university florida, by selecting one of the available modules, we can find the professors who attend the selected cycle, to subsequently create an appointment through a button available in it to be able to create an appointment with the teacher that you select from said module

Cafeteria:

In the Ca section so that we can order them, desserts and drinks are also available, all this would be requested Through Omida Cafeteria we can find two options, Menu to go and Eat in Cafeteria, In the first option we will find the different menus available in cafeteri a form that needs the NIF and choose if it is to take away among other available options in said form, the other option is basic, it would be to reserve a table if our request is to eat in the establishment

Library:

In the Library section we can find two options,Reserve Pecera and reserve portable In Reserve Fishbowl, we can choose which fishbowl to reserve in order to study or talk without disturbing the other students who are in Florida, the NIF will be required And if said fish tank is is available, the same would be for the Reserve Laptop Option, where We will reserve a laptop for our own use, as well as reserving a fish tank, We must identify ourselves by a form with the user's NIF and if the laptop is available

Check course:

In the Florida News section we can find basically all the news that takes place in the University of Florida, this page has a button that translates all the information into English, all type of news will appear in this section
